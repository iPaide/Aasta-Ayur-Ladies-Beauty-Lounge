## WhatsApp Concierge Flow

Opening prompt:

“Welcome to Aasta Ayur Ladies Beauty Lounge. It would be our pleasure to help arrange your visit. Are you looking for Moroccan Bath, hair, skin, nails, bridal beauty, or a curated combination?”

Core branches:

- Moroccan Bath: preferred date and time, first visit or returning guest, solo or group, preparation guidance, optional add-on suggestions.
- Hair: service type, hair length, preferred stylist timing, occasion, add-ons.
- Skin and facials: concern, sensitivity, event date if relevant, treatment recommendation, human handoff for advanced questions.
- Nails and details: manicure/pedicure type, extensions or natural nails, threading/waxing add-ons.
- Bridal and events: event date, group size, location needs, desired services, consultation handoff.
- Offers and packages: current promotions, recommended combinations, availability confirmation.
- Location and hours: address, parking/directions, exact opening hours once confirmed.

Escalation rule:

When the guest asks for medical advice, complex skin concerns, special accessibility needs, confirmed pricing, or a complaint resolution, the automation should acknowledge warmly and route to the team.

Example Moroccan Bath reply:

“We would be happy to arrange your Moroccan Bath experience. May I have your preferred date and time? If you would like, we can also pair it with a facial, hair treatment, or nail service so your visit feels complete.”
